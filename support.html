FAQs

Q: How do I create a new report?

A: To create a new report, log in to your account and click on the "New Report" button.

Q: Can I customize report templates?

A: Yes, you can customize templates in the "Templates" section of your account settings.

Reporting Issues

If you encounter any issues while using RE Report Gen, please email us with the following information:

  • A detailed description of the problem
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Any error messages you received
  • Your account email address

We aim to respond to all support requests within 24 hours.
